1. Log in to the [Dashboard](https://client.falixnodes.net/).

2. Within your server list, choose a server.

3. You will be redirected to your server's [Console page](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/console). Make sure your server is turned off, if not click on the "**Stop**{: .red }" button to the top right of the console.

4. In the top navigation bar, hover over "Manage" then navigate to [File Manager](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/filemanager).

5. Locate your old `server.jar` and click on the three buttons to its right.

6. Delete the file by pressing on "**Delete**{: .red }".

7. In the top navigation bar, hover over "Server" then navigate to [Versions](https://client.falixnodes.net/server/versions).

8. Find your server software, then select a version from the dropdown and click on "**Install**{: .green }".

    > You may also install a custom software, for further information please read our [Custom Jar](/minecraft/java/general/custom-software) article.

Depending on the new software or version, you may need to transfer or convert other files to a different format or re-download your mods or plugins to support the newer software or version. You will also sometimes be required to update your Java version as is described in our [Java Versions](/minecraft/java/general/java-version) guide.
