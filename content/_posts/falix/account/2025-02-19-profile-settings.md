## Introduction
Your profile settings let you customize how your account appears across Falix. This guide covers changing your username, profile picture, pronouns, and other preferences.

## Change Your Username

1. Navigate to [Profile Settings](https://client.falixnodes.net/profile/settings).
2. Find the **Change Username** section.
3. Enter your new username.
4. Confirm the change in the modal that appears.

- Lowercase letters and numbers only
- Must be unique

{: .warning}
> You can only change your username once every **30 days**. If you've changed it recently, you'll see a notice showing when you can change it again.

## Change Your Profile Picture

1. Navigate to [Profile Settings](https://client.falixnodes.net/profile/settings).
2. Click on your current avatar or the **Upload Avatar** button.
3. Drag and drop an image or click to browse your files.
4. Preview your new avatar, then confirm the upload.

**Supported formats:** PNG, JPEG, GIF, WebP, APNG

{: .info}
> Maximum file size is **5 MB**. Animated GIFs and APNGs are supported.

## Set Your Pronouns

1. Navigate to [Profile Settings](https://client.falixnodes.net/profile/settings).
2. Find the **Pronouns** dropdown.
3. Select from the available options:
   - he/him
   - she/her
   - they/them
   - he/they
   - she/they
   - any/all
   - Other (enter custom pronouns, up to 30 characters)

## Other Settings

### External Server Start
Controls whether your servers can be started from external start page. Toggle this on or off from the settings page.

### Crash Notifications
Enable or disable notifications when your server crashes or restarts unexpectedly. Toggle this on or off from the settings page.

### Link Discord Account
Connect your Discord account to your Falix profile:
1. Navigate to [Profile Settings](https://client.falixnodes.net/profile/settings).
2. Find the **Discord** card.
3. Click **Link Discord Account** and authorize the connection.

To unlink, click **Unlink** on the same card.
