## Introduction
You can change your email address or password at any time from your profile settings.

## Change Your Email

1. Navigate to [Profile Settings](https://client.falixnodes.net/profile/settings).
2. Find the **Email** field and enter your new email address.
3. Enter your current password to confirm the change.
4. Click **Save**.
5. You will be logged out and redirected to verify your new email address.

{: .warning}
> Changing your email will log you out of all devices.

## Change Your Password

1. Navigate to [Profile Settings](https://client.falixnodes.net/profile/settings).
2. In the **Change Password** card, fill in:
   - **Old Password** — your current password
   - **New Password** — your new password
   - **Confirm Password** — re-enter your new password
3. Click **Update Password**.

{: .info}
> Changing your password will log you out of all other devices. Only your current session stays active.

## Set a Password

If you signed up using Discord or Google and don't have a password set yet, you'll see a **Set Password** card instead:

1. Navigate to [Profile Settings](https://client.falixnodes.net/profile/settings).
2. Enter your desired password in the **New Password** and **Confirm Password** fields.
3. Click **Set Password**.

{: .info}
> Setting a password is required if you want to use SFTP to manage your server files. You'll be prompted to set one when visiting the SFTP page.
