FalixNodes supports a wide range of games and applications that can be deployed directly from the dashboard.  
This article provides a complete overview of the software currently available to run on Falix servers.

---

## Minecraft

### Minecraft Editions
- **Minecraft Java Edition**
- **Minecraft Bedrock Edition**

### Modded Minecraft
- **Mods** (Forge / Fabric–based modpacks)

---

## Terraria

Falix provides multiple Terraria server options, including modded and plugin-based variants.

- **Terraria (Vanilla)**
- **TShock (Version 5+)**
- **TShock (Version 4 and below)**
- **TModLoader** – `Modded Terraria support`

---

## Factorio

- **Factorio (Vanilla)**
- **Factorio Mod Updater**
- **Clusterio** – `Multi-server Factorio clusters`

---

## Steam Games

Popular Steam-based multiplayer games supported on Falix:

- **Palworld** – `Creature survival`
- **Satisfactory** – `Factory building`
- **ARK: Survival Evolved** – `Dinosaur survival`
- **Rust** – `Autowipe survival`
- **Rust Staging** – `Beta branch`
- **Valheim** – `Viking survival`
- **Valheim Plus** – `Modded Viking`
- **Valheim BepINex** – `Plugin framework`
- **Subnautica** – `Underwater exploration`
- **SCUM** – `Realistic survival`
- **Trackmania** – `Racing`
- **Aloft** – `Sky islands survival`
- **Unturned** – `Zombie survival`
- **Sons of the Forest** – `Horror survival`

---

## Other Game Options

- **Custom Server** – Run almost any game with manual configuration

- **Lua (Luvit)** – `Lua runtime`
